About us

Welcome to Pharma-sas.com! This website provides comprehensive resource for statistical programmers who works in the pharmaceutical industry.It offers a complete review of what entry to advanced level statistical programmers need to know in order to come up with the analysis and reporting of clinical trial data in the pharmaceutical industry.

It is generally believed that SAS programming is the only skill that you have to master. However, other programming knowledge such as VBA, RTF and XML is also needed (though not a must) in order to automate the process of preparing electronic submissions. Moreover, there are a lot of standards and guidelines that SAS programmers should learn due to government guidance for pharmaceutical industry.

Whether you’re a novice seeking an introduction to SAS programming in the pharmaceutical industry or a senior-level programmer exploring new approaches to problem solving, this website offers a lot of practical suggestions that can help you sharpen your skills.

For any questions or suggestions, please feel free to leave a comment. We will reply as soon as possible.
